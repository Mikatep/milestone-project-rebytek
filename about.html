Our Story

Who are we?

Rebytek is a Community Interest Company founded with the primary focus of offering access to technology for vulnerable collectives, while promoting the reutilization and recycling of technological devices.

Why are we?

Rebytek was founded in 2021 in London by a multicultural group of millennials. Rebytek's birth was impelled by the socio-cultural context created by Coronavirus. The multiple restrictions and lockdowns around the globe, and especially in the UK, during covid-19 pandemic spotlighted existent inequalities pertaining the access to technology for non-privileged and vulnerable collectives, and how this inequality impacts negatively these collectives and keeps them from improving their social and economic situation.
