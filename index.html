Our Work

Rebytek is an innovative Community Interest Company (CIC).
We securely collect and refurbish your unwanted or outdated devices, from mobiles phones to laptops, computers or aged IT equipment and donate those devices in good working condition to charitable causes, such as shelters, community centres, orphanages and many more.

Learn More

Another important aspect within the labour of our organization, is the appropriate collection and recycling of those devices that are not working, performing properly or simply are not in good enough condition to be donated.
Our initiative "Techcycle" ensures the proper recycling and reutilization of the savageable parts of those devices, intending to contribute to the reduction of the carbon footprint of e-waste.

About

Rebytek is a Community Interest Company founded with the primary focus of offering access to technology for vulnerable collectives, while promoting the reutilization and recycling of technological devices.
